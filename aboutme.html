About Me

Academic Background

I have a bachelor's degree in physics, mathematics and philosophy. I'm a master's student in pure mathematics, researching topics in category theory and symplectic geometry, but also working in research colaborations in physics and philosophy, in topics such as MathPhys, Cosmology, Logic and Analyctic Philosophy at USP (University of Sao Paulo).
